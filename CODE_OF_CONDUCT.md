# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

QuantForge AI contributors and maintainers must make participation respectful, evidence-led, and
free from harassment. Constructive disagreement about code, research methods, governance, or risk is
welcome. Personal attacks, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized conduct, sustained
disruption, publication of private information, and retaliation are not.

Scientific and security criticism should identify the claim or control, provide reproducible
evidence where possible, and distinguish fact from inference. Financial claims must not be presented
as advice or as validated performance when the repository contains only synthetic evidence.

## Scope

This standard applies in project repositories, review discussions, issue trackers, release channels,
and public spaces where someone represents QuantForge AI.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through an established private channel. Do not
use a public issue when disclosure could expose a target, private data, or a security concern. The
maintainer will acknowledge the report, preserve confidentiality to the extent possible, assess
conflicts of interest, and take proportionate action. Actions may include a private warning, content
removal, temporary participation limits, or a permanent ban.

Knowingly false or retaliatory reports violate this policy. Good-faith reports that are not
substantiated do not.
